A design solution for a global beauty leader.

L'Oréal is a French company specializing in cosmetic products. Founded in 1909, it is now one of the largest beauty companies in the world. The company offers a wide range of makeup, skincare, haircare, fragrance, and much more.


I worked with L'Oréal on the redesign of the user interface (UI) for one of their internal tools. After several workshops with stakeholders, I proposed a more elegant version of the tool, with a dark mode option.
